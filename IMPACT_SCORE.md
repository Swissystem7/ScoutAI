# Impact Score — the ScoutAI signature statistic

A NEW metric: game-changing impact BEYOND the box score. One 0-100 number, transparent components:
- **Grit** — defensive effort/work-rate (pressures, tackles, recoveries)
- **Involvement** — progressive + creative volume
- **Clutch** — high-leverage, game-changing output
- **Energy** — body-language (optional CV layer, off until data exists)

What xG-radars (SmarterScout/Wyscout/StatsBomb IQ) do NOT measure. This is the product IP.
